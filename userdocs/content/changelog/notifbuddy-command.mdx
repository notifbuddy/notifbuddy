---
title: Ask @notifbuddy to open or close a channel
description: Mention @notifbuddy in a Linear comment to open a Slack channel for that issue, or to archive it when the work wraps up — no dashboard trip needed.
date: 2026-07-12
tags: [sync, linear]
---

Channel rules cover the automatic cases — a status change or a matching condition opens the channel for you. For everything else, there's now a manual lever that lives where the conversation already is:

- Comment `@notifbuddy open a channel` on a Linear issue and a Slack channel appears for it, named by your team's template.
- Comment `@notifbuddy close this channel` and the channel archives.

The phrasing is flexible — notifbuddy classifies the intent of the mention rather than matching an exact command string.
